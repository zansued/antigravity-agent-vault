# career-market-optimization-engine

An expert-level career engineering and market analysis skill for ATS optimization and hidden market infiltration.

## Context
Use this skill for high-level career development, CV engineering, and strategic job search tasks.

## Key Features
- **ATS CV Engineering**: Re-engineer master CVs to meet ATS (Applicant Tracking System) standards.
- **Compensation Benchmarking**: Evidence-based analysis of market compensation rates.
- **Hidden Market Infiltration**: Strategies for identifying and accessing jobs not publicly listed.
- **STAR Framework Optimization**: Strategic content re-engineering using the Situation, Task, Action, Result framework.

## How to Use
1. **CV Optimization**: "Optimize my CV for [job title] using STAR framework".
2. **Benchmarking**: "Benchmark my compensation for a [role] in [region]".
3. **Negotiation**: Consult the engine for strategic advice during the interview and offer phases.

## Methodology
Follows a structured workflow from creation (Phase 0) to negotiation (Phase 4).
