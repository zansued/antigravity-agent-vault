# matkorgen-shopping-mcp

An MCP server for grocery shopping in Sweden, enabling product search and cart management.

## Context
Use this skill when you need to search for grocery products, manage shopping carts, or view purchase history from Swedish retailers.

## Key Features
- **Product Search**: Search through vast catalogs of grocery products in Sweden.
- **Cart Management**: Add/remove items and manage multiple shopping sessions.
- **Personalization**: Access favorites and purchase history for faster reordering.
- **Gemini Extension**: Native support for Gemini CLI commands.

## How to Use
1. **Search**: "Find organic milk in the Matkorgen catalog".
2. **Cart**: "Add 2 boxes of cereal to my Matkorgen cart".
3. **Summary**: "Show my shopping cart total and estimated delivery".

## Requirements
- **Region**: Sweden-specific retailers.
- **Runtime**: Node.js/NPM.
