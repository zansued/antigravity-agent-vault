# gemini-supermemory

A real-time learning and knowledge growth extension for Gemini-CLI, leveraging "supermemory" for persistent and evolving agent intelligence.

## Context
Use this skill when you want your agent to remember past interactions, learn new concepts in real-time, and retrieve contextually relevant information from its own persistent knowledge base.

## Key Features
- **Real-Time Learning**: The agent can update its own knowledge base during the conversation.
- **Persistent Memory**: Knowledge persists across sessions, allowing for long-term "growth".
- **Semantic Retrieval**: Advanced retrieval mechanisms for finding the most relevant past information for the current task.
- **Knowledge Growth**: The system is designed to become more specialized and effective the more it is used.

## How to Use
1. **Store**: "Learn the following architectural pattern: [PatternDescription]".
2. **Retrieve**: "Recall everything we discussed regarding the [ProjectName] database schema".
3. **Analyze**: "Based on your current knowledge of [Subject], suggest an optimization for [Task]".

## Requirements
- **Runtime**: Gemini CLI.
- **Memory**: Requires a configured supermemory backend/storage.
