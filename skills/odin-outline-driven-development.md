# odin-outline-driven-development

An extension implementing the Outline Driven development (ODIN) approach for building and managing agentic intelligence.

## Context
Use this skill to apply the ODIN methodology to your development process, ensuring that your AI agent follows a structured, outline-based approach to complex tasks.

## Key Features
- **Outline-Driven Intelligence**: Prioritizes structured outlines for task breakdown and execution.
- **Methodological Framework**: Follows the ODIN approach to modularize and organize agentic logic.
- **Official Integration**: Now a recognized component of the Gemini CLI ecosystem.
- **Scalable Architecture**: Designed for handling increasingly complex development projects through hierarchical outlines.

## How to Use
1. **Plan**: "Create an ODIN outline for the [ProjectScope]".
2. **Execute**: "Follow the ODIN outline to implement the next phase of the project".
3. **Refine**: "Update the ODIN outline based on the latest architectural requirements".

## Requirements
- **Methodology**: Requires understanding of the "Outline Driven" development philosophy.
- **Runtime**: Gemini CLI.
