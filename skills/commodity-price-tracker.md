# commodity-price-tracker

An extension for Gemini CLI providing real-time data on oil, gas, and other commodity prices via the OilPriceAPI.

## Context
Use this skill when you need current or historical pricing data for energy commodities, market analysis, or economic research within your agent's workflow.

## Key Features
- **Real-Time Pricing**: Access to live prices for Crude Oil (WTI, Brent), Natural Gas, and refined petroleum products.
- **Historical Data**: Retrieval of historical price trends and data points for market analysis.
- **Commodity Coverage**: Also includes data for various other global commodities and energy indices.
- **Natural Language Queries**: Supports queries like "What is the current price of Brent crude?" or "Show me the natural gas price trend for the last week".

## How to Use
1. **Query**: "Get the latest price for [CommodityName] from OilPriceAPI".
2. **Analyze**: "Compare the price of WTI vs Brent for the current month".
3. **Report**: "Generate a summary of natural gas price fluctuations over the last 30 days".

## Requirements
- **API**: Requires an active OilPriceAPI key.
- **Runtime**: Gemini CLI.
